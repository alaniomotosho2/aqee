Hajj and Umrah Advice
With million of pilgrims visiting Mecca and Medinah every year, facilities are now better than ever.It is very different to the Hajj and Umrah that our grand parents performed.Authorities work hard to make your pilgrimage as easy as possible. There are many free shower and washroom facilities. Cold water for drinking is available
Hotels offer all the luxuries you need to recooperate any lost energy. Transport is on par with what you would excpect from any major city.
The climate ranges from between warm to very hot, depending on the time of the year. There is very little rainfall For this reason it is always best to think summer when packing
Remember what you are there for and try to spend as much of your time in remebrance of Allah (SWT).Engage in recitation and Dua. Try to attain as much knowledge as you can about the beauty of Islam while you are in this very special place. This way you will get the most rewarding experince for your pilgrimage
Although the authorities of Saudi Arabia do an excellent job in ensuring that Hajj and Umrah experience is as easy as possible, the pilgrimage itself is quite demanding and involves quite a lot of physical exertions. It is always good to perform it when in generally good health
