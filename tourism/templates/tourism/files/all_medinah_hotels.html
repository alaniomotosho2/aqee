Hajj and Umrah Advice
With million of pilgrims visiting Mecca and Medinah every year, facilities are now better than ever.It is very different to the Hajj and Umrah that our grand parents performed.Authorities work hard to make your pilgrimage as easy as possible. There are many free shower and washroom facilities. Cold water for drinking is available
Hotels offer all the luxuries you need to recooperate any lost energy. Transport is on par with what you would excpect from any major city.
The climate ranges from between warm to very hot, depending on the time of the year. There is very little rainfall For this reason it is always best to think summer when packing
Remember what you are there for and try to spend as much of your time in remebrance of Allah (SWT).Engage in recitation and Dua. Try to attain as much knowledge as you can about the beauty of Islam while you are in this very special place. This way you will get the most rewarding experince for your pilgrimage
Although the authorities of Saudi Arabia do an excellent job in ensuring that Hajj and Umrah experience is as easy as possible, the pilgrimage itself is quite demanding and involves quite a lot of physical exertions. It is always good to perform it when in generally good health

All Hotels in Medinah

DAR AL TAQWA

Just foot steps away from the main entrance to the Holy Mosque, the Dar Al Taqwa Royal Marquis by Eclipse Madinah enjoys one of the city's most enviable locations. Its close proximity to the entrance of the Mosque makes it the perfect hotel for the many pilgrims and visitors who perform Hajja nd Umrah each year. It is also just a short 15 minute drive from Madinah Airport. The hotel consists of 13 floors and is equipped with 7 elevators aimed at the ensuring the easy flow of guests to conveniently access the Holy Mosque in front. Secured underground car parking is available for the hotel guests. Should you wish to relax and avail a rent-a-car for your local transportation, car rental desk is availble within the hotel. A fully equipped Business center provides full secretarial services, computer workstations, faxing and photocopying if you are in Madinah on a business trip. The hotel also offers an in-house Laundry facilities for our guests with same day delivery and 'express delivery' as required. Luggage assistance is offered by our own professionally trained staff who may also offer you some local tips on places to go which may not be in your agenda, making it yet another memorable experience.

DAR AL IMAN INTERCONTINENTAL

THE CITY'S BEST LOCATION Dar Al Iman InterContinental Madinah occupies a choice spot on the piazza of the Holy Masjid Mosque of the Prophet Mohammed. Surrounded by the city’s financial, commercial and shopping districts, the hotel is also within easy reach of historic sites including Jannat-ul-Baqi cemetery, the Badar Battlefield, Ohud Mountain, Masjid Al Qiblatain and Masjid Al Quba.

DYAR INTERNATIONAL

5* 50 meters from Prophet's Mosque Positioned within the heart of the central city, the Dyar International Hotel Madinah is just a short walk from the Masjid Al-Nabawi (Mosque of the Prophet Mohammad). This means it is also convenient for those who want to tour the Jannatul Baqi or take in the excellent shopping of the area too. The Dyar International Hotel Madinah is located at Markazia (Central) Area - Harram Al-Madina-Al-Munawara, and is 7.5 miles from the Prince Mohammad Bin Abdulaziz Airport.

ANWAR MADINAH MOVENPICK

Madinah’s largest hotel with extensive facilities for families, individual travellers and leisure groups. The closest hotel to the Prophet’s Mosque, it is close to all main attractions and directly linked to the shopping mall with underground parking.

HILTON MADINAH

The Madinah Hilton Hotel is located only a short walk from the Holy Mosque, right in the heart of Madinah's shopping district. The hotel has lovely views of the Holy Mosque.
